ABC Technologies Employee Handbook

Company Overview
ABC Technologies is a global software development company specializing in AI, Cloud Computing, and Data Analytics solutions.

Working Hours
Employees are expected to work from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM Monday through Friday.

Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to:
- 12 Casual Leaves per year
- 10 Sick Leaves per year
- 15 Paid Vacation Leaves annually

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Code of Conduct
All employees must maintain professional behavior and follow ethical guidelines.

IT Support
For technical issues employees should contact:
itsupport@abctech.com

Performance Evaluation
Employee performance reviews are conducted every 6 months.

Promotion Policy
Promotions are based on performance, leadership skills, and business contribution.

Security Policy
Employees must not share company confidential data externally.

Training Programs
ABC Technologies provides AI and Cloud certification training programs for employees.
